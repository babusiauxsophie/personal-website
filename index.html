Projects

De Noodcentrale

For the course @work2, my group, consisting of myself and two other students, developed an application for the Emergency Center of East and West Flanders. The Emergency Center has staff members who need to listen to recordings of emergency calls in order to provide feedback to the operators. We built it so that they can access these reviews per operator and use this feedback during performance evaluations.

  • Express
  • Handlebars
  • bcrypt

Parkieten Ringen Backoffice

As part of our school project for the @work3 course, my classmate and I developed a comprehensive back-office solution tailored for Luc, a dedicated 72-year-old member of the Belgische Vereniging van Parkieten- en Papegaaienliefhebbers vzw. Luc manages the ordering of rings for these birds, a task that he has traditionally handled using binders and Excel sheets. Our goal was to simplify and streamline his workflow, ensuring efficiency while keeping the system easy to navigate and use for someone of his age with limited computer skills.

  • DDEV
  • Docker
  • MySQL

R4 Club

For my Data Management course, I was tasked with developing a website with a database for a niche community. I chose to focus on the R4 Club of Belgium, a group of Renault 4 enthusiasts. I aimed to create a platform where members could interact and connect with one another. This project not only allowed me to gain experience working with Laravel but also sparked my interest in further developing the site in my own time.

Clar Development

For the Web Animations course, I was tasked with recreating the website of Clar Development, a site filled with dynamic web animations. Working on this project really opened my eyes to how much more is possible in front-end development. It demonstrated how animation can transform a static website into something engaging, interactive, and visually captivating, enhancing the overall user experience.

StudioBoost

For the UX/UI Prototyping course, I developed a website based on the designs provided to me. This was my first real experience analyzing design layouts and translating them into functional templates.
